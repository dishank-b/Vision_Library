# Markov

## Markov Property

The likelihood of a future state depends on present state only. 

$$
P(X_t|X_{t-1}, X_{t-2},...,X_1) = P(X_t|X_{t-1})
$$

## Markov Chain

A stochastic process with Markov property.

## Hidden Markov Model \(HMM\)
